Misinterpretation

People often quote the famous remark of Ben Franklin, “[t]hey who can give up essential liberty to obtain a little temporary safety, deserve neither liberty nor safety”, to criticize governments’ attempts of storing digital data generated by netizens, especially for those people living in West Europa, where privacy is regarded as one of the most important human rights. However, not to mention the justice of such measures taken by governements, there is perhaps a misinterpretation of the remark of Franklin made by people who quoted it.
